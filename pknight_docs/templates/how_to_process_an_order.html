Shopping Guide

Page One: How to Place an Order

  • Choose the country of shipment, product specifications, and quantity.
  • You have three buttons to choose from:
    • Add To Cart: Use this to add the product to your shopping cart. This is usually for purchasing multiple items.
    • BUY IT NOW: Use this to go directly to the checkout page.
    • Inquiry Now: Use this to send an email for product inquiries or to request a discount code.

Page Two: Shopping Cart

  • You can modify the quantity of the products in your cart or remove them entirely.
  • In the ORDER SUMMARY section, you can see the total cost of the items you're purchasing.
  • You have three buttons to choose from:
    • Proceed To Checkout: Use this to go to the detailed checkout page where you can choose your payment method and billing and shipping addresses.
    • Continue Shopping: Use this to go back to the product page on the website, where you can continue to add other products to your cart.
    • PayPal: Use this to skip the detailed checkout page and directly proceed with PayPal as your payment method.

Page Three: Checkout Details

  • If you still wish to use PayPal, you can click on the Paypal in red section.
  • If you want to pay by credit card, please use the blue section.
    • Fill in your shipping address.
    • Choose your shipping method.
    • Select your payment method; here, choose Pay by "credit/debit card and more"
    • If you have a discount code, please enter it in the section on the right and click "apply" to use your discount code.
